# Pricing

> Transparent, usage-based pricing for all AI models. Pay only for what you use with no hidden fees

WisGate offers flexible, pay-as-you-go pricing that scales with your usage. There are no monthly commitments, setup fees, or hidden costs. You only pay for the API calls you make.

> **View pricing on our website**: Check the latest pricing information and model availability at [https://wisgate.ai/pricing](https://wisgate.ai/pricing)

## Pricing model

WisGate uses a **usage-based pricing model** where you're charged per API request based on:

* **Model type** - Different models have different pricing tiers
* **Input tokens** - The number of tokens in your request
* **Output tokens** - The number of tokens in the response
* **Request type** - Chat completions, embeddings, image generation, etc.

> **Tip:** All pricing is displayed in USD. You can view real-time usage and costs in your dashboard. Billing occurs at the end of each billing cycle.

## How billing works

### Billing process

1. **Usage tracking** - All API calls are tracked in real-time in your dashboard
2. **Billing cycle** - Charges are calculated at the end of each calendar month
3. **Automatic invoicing** - Invoices are generated and sent to your email automatically
4. **Payment processing** - Charges are processed using your saved payment method

### Billing characteristics

* **Pay-as-you-go** - Scale up or down as needed
* **Transparent pricing** - See exact costs before and after each API call

### Payment methods

We accept the following payment methods:

* Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
* Debit cards
* Bank transfer (for enterprise customers)

## Pricing FAQ

### How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is calculated based on the number of tokens processed (input + output) and the specific model used. Each model has its own pricing tier. You can see the exact cost breakdown in your dashboard after each API call.

### Do you offer refunds?

API usage charges are non-refundable as they represent consumed services. For billing disputes or questions about prepaid balance refunds, please contact our support team.

### Can I get a discount for high volume?

Yes! Enterprise customers with consistent high-volume usage can qualify for custom pricing and volume discounts. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements.

### What happens if I exceed my budget?

You can set budget limits in your dashboard. When a limit is reached, API calls will be paused until you increase your budget or add more credits. You'll receive email notifications before reaching your limit.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. You only pay for API usage based on the published pricing. There are no setup fees, monthly subscriptions, or additional charges.
